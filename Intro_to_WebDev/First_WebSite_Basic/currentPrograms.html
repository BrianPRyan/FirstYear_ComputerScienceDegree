Volunteering with The Society of Saint Vincent de Paul gives you an opportunity to make all the difference in a persons life. Our 11,000 volunteers from across the country are people from all walks of life who are helping to change the lives of people in need. Whether you have a few hours to spare or would like to make a more long-term commitment, there are lots of ways you can get involved and help change a familys life. Visiting people at home, in hospitals and in prisons. Providing practical support and information to people who contact us. Supporting our range of specialist services such as hostels for people who are out of home; day care services for older people; resource centres; and holiday centresVolunteering at one of our large network of charity shops. Working with schools and third level colleges to bring awareness of the work of SVP and the value of volunteering to young people. As a first step you can contact your local office or apply online. You will get more information on the work we do and be brought through an application process that helps us to match your talents, interests and availability to our work with people in need.
Right now thousands of people throughout Ireland are struggling to provide food, heat and clothing for themselves and their families. Whether you want to volunteer, fundraise for us, make a donation or get involved in one of our campaigns, there are loads of ways you can help. However you choose to get involved, your help is invaluable in making all the difference to the lives of people in your community.
